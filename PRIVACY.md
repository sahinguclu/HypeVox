# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 28, 2026

HypeVox does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or any other information from its users.

## Data Storage

All user preferences — including theme (light/dark), selected language, and equalizer preset — are stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your browser and is never accessible to the developer or any third party.

## No Tracking

HypeVox does not use:
- Analytics or crash reporting
- Tracking cookies or pixels
- Third-party services or APIs
- Telemetry of any kind
- External network requests

## Audio Processing

All audio processing (volume amplification and equalization) is performed entirely **on your device** using the Web Audio API. No audio data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Changes

If this policy changes in the future, users will be notified via an update to this document.
